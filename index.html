About

I am a PhD student working on the ATLAS detector. My research focuses on Higgs pair production and machine learning for event classification. I like building reproducible analysis workflows and writing tutorial style docs that help others get started quickly.

Current interests: HH in multi lepton final states, neutral particle flow isolation, robust parameterizations (kappa, EFT, STXS), and practical tools for training and evaluation.
